As members of the University's staff, you are cordially invited to attend the 1983 Annual Staff Breakfast presented by President James Zumberge and the Staff Assembly.
The continental breakfast and get-together will be held in the Town and Gown Auditorium (on Main Campus) at 8:30 AM on Thursday 11/3.
This is an opportunity to meet some of the other staff members affiliated with the University, as well as the Staff Assembly representatives and President Zumberge.
